Season's Greetings from BROOKVILLE!

As 2018 comes to a close, the BROOKVILLE team is reflecting on our 100th year of operations - one of our most monumental to date. We could not have achieved this success without the support of our customers and key stakeholders in every industry we serve.

Thank you for believing in us and giving us the opportunity to serve you each and every day. We hope that you and yours have a holiday season filled with joy and happiness, and we look forward to continuing to serve you in the New Year.

For more on BROOKVILLE's big year in 2018, please hover over or tap the photos below!

In the Spring of 2018, BROOKVILLE delivered its 25th restored PCC Streetcar to San Francisco.
BROOKVILLE recently delivered five Liberty Streetcars to the City of Milwaukee. The streetcar line opened on Nov. 2, 2018.
BROOKVILLE is working on the last of six PCC streetcar restorations and modernizations for the City of El Paso. The streetcar line opened on Nov. 9, 2018.
BROOKVILLE recently delivered seven Liberty Streetcars to Oklahoma City. The new streetcar system opened for revenue service on Dec. 14, 2018.
BROOKVILLE is wrapping up the last of eight GP35 locomotive rebuilds for Metro-North Railroad in New York City.
In August 2018, BROOKVILLE hosted a 100th Anniversary celebration. Over 800 guests attended, including employees and their families.
BROOKVILLE continued to deliver new and rebuilt mining equipment for its customers across North America.

Happy Holidays!
